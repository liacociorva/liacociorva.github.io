Popular Dishes

Some famous dishes include bibimbap, a mixed rice bowl usually topped with a protein and vegetables, bulgogi, a barbequed beef, and japchae, a stir-fried noodle dish.

Cuisine

Korean cuisine is based largely on vegetables, rice, and meats. Famous elements of this include Korean Barbeque, and popular foods such as kimchi.
