Introduction

Welcome to my website! Here you will learn about popular and well-known foods around a few different locations in Asia, including China, Japan, India, Vietnam, and Korea. You will not only learn a bit about the type of foods that the countries are famous for, but you will also see examples of dishes that are well-known, not only throughout Asia, but universally. The regions included in this website cover the most famous of regional cuisines: East Asian, Southeast Asian, South Asian, Central Asian, and Middle Eastern. A cuisine is a characteristic style of cooking practices and traditions, usually associated with a specific culture. Asia, being the largest and most populous continent, is home to many cultures, many of which have their own characteristic cuisine.

Ingredients common to many cultures in the East and Southeast regions of the continent include rice, ginger, garlic, sesame seeds, chilies, dried onions, soy, and tofu. Stir frying, steaming, and deep frying are common cooking methods.
