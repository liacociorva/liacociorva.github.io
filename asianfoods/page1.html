Popular Dishes

Popular dishes include dumplings, wontons, chow mein, and fried rice.

Cuisine

Types of Chinese cuisine include Shandong (famous for seafood), Hunan (famous for hot and icy flavor), Zhejiang (also seafood), Jiangsu (famous for being royal cuisine), Fujian (famous for soup), Sichuan (known for spicy flavors), Anhui (famous for using lots of vegetables), and Cantonese (famous for stir fry).
